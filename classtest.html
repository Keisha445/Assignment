TRAVIS SCOTT

Travis Scott, born Jacques Bermon Webster II on April 30, 1991, in Houston, Texas, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He has achieved significant milestones throughout his career. Below is a table summarizing some of his notable achievements:

Year Achievements
2012 Signed first major label deal with Epic Records and Kanye West's GOOD Music.
2013 Signed a record deal with T.I.'s Grand Hustle imprint.
2014 Released mixtape "Days Before Rodeo," which became the highest-selling mixtape in history by 2024.
2015 Released debut studio album "Rodeo," debuting at number three on the US Billboard 200 chart.
2016 Released second studio album "Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight," achieving his first No. 1 album.
2018 Released third studio album "Astroworld," receiving critical acclaim and commercial success.
2023 Released fourth studio album "Utopia."
